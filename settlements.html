TANAYU ARCHIPELAGOS

Tanayu naturally form groups called archipelagos. There is a main Tanayu that guides the group along a migratory route. Some nations live on these Tanayu and travel with them. They have learned to harvest the tadpole algae without compromising the ecosystem for food and fuel, and have mastered the art of fishing. They study the path of their archipelago and which resources the ocean floor will make available for them at each point.
