Yhizago: the call of the tanayu

Welcome to Nero-3D2Y, also known as Yhizago (why-zuh-go) /'waɪ.zə.goʊ/, a planet completely covered by water. Blossoming with alien fauna and flora, there is a species that seems to be completely out of place. They call themselves human. Dive into this world to see how they manage to survive, and maybe try to piece together their story.
This is a world building project that includes constructed languages, speculative biology, politics, history, conflicts, and much more.
